WHO I AM

Home

My name is Charles W. Reid Jr. I was born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania on July 1, 1983. I have always been enamored with technology and computers, so much so that as a very young child my parents and grandparents would often ask me to do things that they didn't quite understand, such as set up the VCR.

I briefly attended Penn State University, majoring in Computer Science from 2000 to 2001. I am a former pharmacy professional, working in that industry for over 6 years. Recently moving to LA with my girlfriend, I am seeking my first web development opportunity. I have never lost my love for gadgets and devices, and I feel that the current innovations of web and mobile technology provide the perfect outlet for me to pursue my interests.
